Casa Del Chelo

For nature lovers seeking a break from city life, Casa del Chelo, located in Tagaytay, beckons as the ideal retreat. This peaceful hideaway provides the ideal location to relax and rejuvenate within the calming embrace of nature. It is surrounded by stunning surroundings.

Casa del Chelo offers a private retreat for both family togetherness and barkada vacations. It is a paradise created specifically for family and friends. It offers breathtaking views from its lovely hilltop location, creating the perfect environment for priceless memories. Casa del Chelo makes sure that your getaway is a seamless fusion of rest and connection, whether you're soaking in the luxury of your own private staycation home, partaking in activities inspired by nature, or just taking in the beauty of the surrounds.

We Share a Sense of Concern

At Casa del Chelo, we make a sincere commitment to looking after our visitors. Every element of your stay is given top priority from the time you enter our refuge to guarantee that you have an unmatched level of comfort and relaxation. Our devoted team is at your disposal at all times, prepared to meet your needs and go above and beyond your expectations. Our ultimate objective is to create a refuge where you feel really cared for and appreciated during your stay, whether it's the precise attention to your rooms, the culinary delights made with care, or the tranquil ambience we arrange.
